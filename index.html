My favorite hobby
Skating

It gives pleasure and stirs up emotions. It strengthens muscles and when done by listening to music, is energizing and exciting. That is why I like skating so much. I always listen to music when roller-skating and I feel great. It's Great Exercise. Most of us don't get as much physical activity as we should for optimal health. Roller skating is a perfect way to change that, because it provides a complete aerobic workout, but will be fun enough that you barely notice. Plus, it's easy on your joints.
